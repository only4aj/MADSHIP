FAQs

How long does shipping take ?

Shipping typically takes 2-3 working days, although delivery times may vary depending on your location. Rest assured, we make every effort to get your order to you as quickly as possible.

Is My Personal Information Secure ?

We take data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and protected using industry-standard security measures.

What payment methods are accepted ?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets like PayPal, and even offer the convenience of cash on delivery for eligible orders.

How Can I Contact Customer Support ?

You can find detailed contact information, including our email address and phone number, on our Contact Page. We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Can I cancel my order anytime ?

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase by contacting our customer support. We understand that circumstances may change, and we're here to assist you with any cancellation requests during this timeframe.
